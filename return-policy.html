How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, simply contact us within 30 days of your purchase date. You can do so by:

To be eligible for a refund, your request must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the original purchase date. Refunds are available to both individual purchasers and organisations purchasing on behalf of their teams.
